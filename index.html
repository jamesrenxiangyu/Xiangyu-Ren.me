I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical Computer Engineering at the University of Victoria, BC, Canada, advised by Dr. Lin Cai. I first joined CNLab in 2019 as a M.Sc. student and later transferred to the Ph.D. program in 2020. I obtained my B.Eng in Automation Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), Chengdu, China. My current research interest focus on the analysis of communication networks and protocol design. I am especially interested in Deterministic Networking (DetNet) and Software Defined Network (SDN) development and application.

Interests

  • Protocol design and model anlaysis in Communication Networks
  • Deterministic Networking, Software Defined Network
  • Vehicular Networks, LEO Space Network
